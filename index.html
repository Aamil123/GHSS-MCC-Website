About School

The city of Kozhikode speaks volumes about its illustrious past and resplendent future. Being located at the heart of the small panoramic village called Kovoor in Kozhikode city Govt Higher Secondary School Medical College Campus is one of the most eminent schools of the city. This particular State funded school established in the year 1965 was primarily a single teacher insititution. It has grown abundantly over the years and is now approaching the zennith of its glory

Head Master's Message Kunnan

GHSS Medical College Campus, which is part of the Prism project in the North Mandal of Kozhikode, is a university where more than 3000 students study. The university has introduced projects that provide comprehensive development to each student. It has been in existence for some time now, keeping abreast of the latest developments to disseminate news about multifaceted talents. With modern facilities, the university is advancing as a center sanctioned by the government.
